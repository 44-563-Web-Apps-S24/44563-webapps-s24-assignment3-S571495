Steve Yellow the Sheep

Meet Steve Yellow, a swift and mysterious Sheep with a sleek colored coat. He prowls the mystical jungles, blending seamlessly with the lush greenery.

Favorite Games of Steve Yellow

  • Stealthy Stalk - A game of sneaking through the jungle undetected.
  • Jade Jump - Competing with other jaguars in high jumps across the treetops.
  • Pounce Puzzles - Solving puzzles to catch his prey with precision.
